Instagram Picture Feed Test

Here is what you need to do to participate:
1. Install the Instagram app
For iPhone and iPad, launch the iTunes App Store    For Android, launch Google Play Store
Search for Instagram and you will find this.    Click on Install.
2. Create an Instagram account
Launch the Instagram app and choose to register using your email address or Facebook account.
3. Take a picture
Once registered, click on this icon on the main screen to snap a picture.

4. Tag and upload the picture
On my Android device, I need to click the paperclip icon to allow Instagram to use the pictures I take.
In the Add Description field, be sure to include #nhs84 along with any other details.
Click the checkmark in upper right corner to upload it.
5. View all the pictures
Cick on your profile icon   to see the pictures you've uploaded.
You can also click on Search   then click on Tags and enter #nhs84 to see all other submitted photos.

NOTE: Although the pictures appear on Instagram moments later, it can take up to 30 minutes before they appear in the pool of shared images below.  Also refer to Instagram's help page for additional information.
